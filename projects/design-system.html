About The App

This app is an interactive page that gives users a walkthrough of a certain topic ( In this case: The Sex Pistols). The page is meant to be sort of a digital gallery walk-through of their careers, members and history with related topics.

The app will include queued sounds ( when scrolling past a certain point), interactive imagery, spotify playlists, a shopping panel with links leading to amazon (mimicking the listings), disappearing text and animation, and videos that play when a certain point of the page is reached.
